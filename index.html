What is sennus?

Oculus Animi Index

The eye looks but it is the mind that sees.

sen•nus is an amalgamation of the words Sentio & Magnus. It roughly translates to & carries the directive to Feel and Perceive Big.

The function of sennus is focused around learning & growth and producing tasteful results in the process.

In this age of many many(many) design languages coexisting, sennus is an idea which promotes the choice and use of a clean design language, to emphasize upon the quality and finer details.


General ideas

• Respecting visual hierarchy

• Controlled contrast for legibility

• Logically & reasonably selected colour palette

Logo & Iconography

• Scalability

• Digital & Print & Merch friendly

• Tasteful use of negative spaces

• Simple yet meaningful geometric construction

Web presence

• Mobile first/optimized

• Modular grid layout

• Designing for thumbs

• Minimal animations where necessary

• Prioritzing infusion of the brand's identity into the UI
