What is Sennus?

SEN•NUS is an amalgamation of the words Sentio & Magnus. It roughly translates to & carries the directive to Feel and Perceive Big.

Sennus is a pet project where I hope to practice, sharpen and showcase my front-end development skillsets!

Last but not the least, in this age of a multiverse of design languages, sennus is an idea which promotes the use of clean design language, to emphasize upon the quality and finer details over loud sensory excitement, on steroids.



General

•negative space

•controlled contrast

•1 / 2 hue colour palette

Logo

•avoiding gradient

•simple geometric construction

•scalability

Web presence

•infusing a strong emotion in the brand concept

•mobile optimized grid layout

•minimal animations where necessary
